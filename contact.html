How to Reach Out

If you're interested in joining or collaborating with us, we warmly welcome you to get in touch, even if you're not a Sogang University student. Please send a short self-introduction, your CV, and your transcript to: sunoklee@sogang.ac.kr

Applications for undergraduate internships and master's programs are welcome.

For master's applicants, please review the program details first: Graduate Program Information, then contact us.
