The Future Experience Design (FED) Group is founded on the principle of Human-Centered Design (HCD), driving research to shape a better, more equitable future for all. Our mission is to transform emerging technologies into meaningful human experiences. We achieve this by actively enabling intuitive engagement with future technologies, fostering innovative designs that facilitate effective communication with the world, and fundamentally ensuring that the benefits of technology are shared by every individual equally, thereby promoting digital inclusion and equity.
