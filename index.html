About FED Lab

FED Lab is founded on Human-Centered Design principles. We study how people can intuitively engage with future technologies and how design can promote equitable access.

Research Areas

Human-AI Interaction

Designing interfaces and workflows that make AI understandable, trustworthy, and actionable.

Inclusive UX

Building experiences that support diverse users, contexts, and abilities.

Future Prototyping

Rapidly prototyping new interaction paradigms to validate future scenarios.
