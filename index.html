The Future Experience Design Lab (FED Lab) is founded on the principle of Human-Centered Design (HCD), driving research to shape a better, more equitable future for all. Our mission is to transform emerging technologies into meaningful human experiences. We achieve this by actively enabling intuitive engagement with future technologies, fostering innovative designs that facilitate effective communication with the world, and fundamentally ensuring that the benefits of technology are shared by every individual equally, thereby promoting digital inclusion and equity.

How can humans and technology collaborate each other in an intended way?

To answer the question, we explore

How systems operate, respond, and shape interaction

We investigate the internal logic and behavioral patterns of technological systems, including how they respond, guide interaction, and influence human experience. This helps us approach technology not only as a tool, but also as an active agent in interaction.

How people think, interpret, and experience technology

We study the cognitive and reflective processes through which people perceive, interpret, and engage with technology. Our work explores how users form expectations, assign meaning, and negotiate their relationships with emerging technological systems.

Our goal is to envision futures in which technological systems support more meaningful, responsible, and human-centered experiences.
